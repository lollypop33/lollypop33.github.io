Data Retention
Only the last 90 days worth of interface messages are typically retained in the Data Management Application. Any messages greater than 90 days old are automatically purged by the integration engine. This is based on an expected volume of 10,000 messages per day. If your organization anticipates higher or lower volume, the number of days worth of data to retain can be adjusted; contact your Commure representative.
