Moving Between Options
While viewing an option, there are usually links that you can click to view more detailed information about a particular item that is displayed on the current screen. For example, from the Messages option, you can click a link to view the Message Details for a specific message. Once you have drilled down to a detail screen, you can then do any of the following:
•To go back to the previous screen (Messages in our example), click the breadcrumb links located at the top of the page, or click the Back button on your browser.
•To go to a different option, open the sidebar and select the desired option (see Sidebar).
