Other Ways to Get Help
•Get help from your system administrator. If you have a problem running the application, first report the issue to your hospital's Strongline administrator.
•Get help from Commure Support. If your administrator cannot solve your issue, send an e-mail to commure_support@commure.com. Include your contact information (your name, telephone number, e-mail address, office or cell number, and best time to call). Please include as many details about your issue as possible, such as which application you’re using, whether you are a desktop or mobile device user (indicate the type of device), version information, error messages, and who is affected. Commure Support will respond by the next business day. Telephone support is available 8:00 am to 8:00 pm EST, Monday through Friday (excluding regular business holidays), at 888-994-2443.
